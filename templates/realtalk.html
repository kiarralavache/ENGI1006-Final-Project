The Real Talk Inititative

(Student Diversity Leadership Conference) in Atlanta, GA. It was one of
the best experiecnces of my life and I learned so much from being there.
When I came back, I wanted to try and bring some of what I learned back
to my high school community. With the help of some friends, I started
