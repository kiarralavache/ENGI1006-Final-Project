Why I Decided To Drop Pre-Med

I decided to drop Pre-Med during the Fall 2019 semester. One of the
course requirements for medical school is physics lab. I had the option
to take the physics lab offered in Columbia College, but I took the
SEAS one instead because it fit into my schedule better. I was really
struggling in this class and it made me question if the amount of
stress that I was under was worth it. After having multiple ~DEEP~
conversaitons with one of my mentors, I realized that medical school isn't
really what I want to do with my life. Now that I've come to this
realization, I think I'm more interested in getting a degree in Business
and/or Public Health.
